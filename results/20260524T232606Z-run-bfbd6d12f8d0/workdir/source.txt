Excerpt from the 2024 Marsh Reserve annual report:

"The Marsh Reserve recorded 412 butterfly species in 2023, down
from 437 in 2022. Restoration work began in spring 2024 with the
planting of native wildflowers along the eastern boundary. The
reserve will host its 50th-anniversary celebration in September
2026, the formal opening of which will be presided over by the
honorary chair, Dr. Anaya Holm."
