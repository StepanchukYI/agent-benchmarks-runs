The Acme Widget Co. quarterly report for Q2 2025 lists three product
lines: the original Acme Widget, the Acme Mini, and the Acme Pro. The
report breaks out revenue per line as follows.

  - Acme Widget:  $4.2M
  - Acme Mini:    $1.1M
  - Acme Pro:     $3.7M

The report also describes customer-acquisition cost, churn rate, and
gross margin in aggregate across all lines, but does not break those
metrics out per product line.
