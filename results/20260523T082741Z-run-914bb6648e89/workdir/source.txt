In a 2023 interview, the architect Iren Olbrich said about her
unbuilt Helsinki harbour pavilion:

"The form follows the water — every angle in the roof is a tangent
to a wave I watched one November morning."

Critics later described the design as "a frozen tide of timber",
though Olbrich herself never used that phrase. The pavilion remains
unrealized after the harbour authority withdrew the site lease in
2024 citing zoning concerns.
